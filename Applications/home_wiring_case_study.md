🏠 Case Study: Simple Home Wiring Calculation

🧠 Problem

A home appliance operates at:

- Voltage = 230V
- Current = 2A

Find:

1. Power consumed
2. Suitable resistance
3. What happens if multiple appliances are connected in parallel

---

⚡ Solution

1. Power Calculation

P = V × I
P = 230 × 2 = 460 Watts

---

2. Resistance Calculation

R = V / I
R = 230 / 2 = 115 Ohms

---

3. Parallel Connection Insight

- Each appliance gets same voltage
- Total current increases
- Equivalent resistance decreases
