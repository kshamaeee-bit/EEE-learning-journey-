## Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL)

KVL states that:

The sum of all voltages in a closed loop is equal to zero.

Formula:
Sum of voltages = 0

Understanding:
- A loop is any closed path in a circuit
- Voltage gains = Voltage drops

Example:
If a loop has:
+10V (battery) and -4V, -6V (drops)

Then:
10 - 4 - 6 = 0
