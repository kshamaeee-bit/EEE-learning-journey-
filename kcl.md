## Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL)

KCL states that:

The total current entering a node is equal to the total current leaving the node.

Formula:
Sum of incoming currents = Sum of outgoing currents

Understanding:
- A node is a junction where multiple branches meet
- Current is conserved at a node

Example:
If 3A and 2A enter a node,
Then total incoming = 5A

So, outgoing current must also be 5A
