## Ohm's Law in Real Life

Ohm's Law is not just a formula, it is used in real electrical systems.

Example: Mobile Charger

A mobile charger controls voltage and current to safely charge a phone.

- If voltage is too high → device damage
- If resistance changes → current changes

Using Ohm’s Law:
I = V / R

Engineers design circuits so that current stays within safe limits.

Understanding:
- Every electronic device follows Ohm’s Law
- It helps prevent overheating and damage
